US City Explorer Offline

This is a folder-based offline web app using your U.S. map image as the base.

Files:
- index.html
- styles.css
- data.js
- app.js
- us_map_base.png

How to use:
1. Keep all files together in the same folder.
2. Open index.html in your browser.
3. Everything works offline.

Notes:
- State click zones are approximate overlays placed on top of your image.
- City locations are bundled into the app for search and zoom behavior.
- Search suggestions separate same-name cities by state and county.
